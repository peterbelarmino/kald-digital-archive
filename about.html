About the Digital Archive

Preserving KALD's institutional memory, historical media, and educational records.

Purpose

The KALD Digital Archive Center was developed to organize and preserve photographs, videos, documents, reports, and historical materials related to learning differences awareness and education in Kuwait.

Archive Scope

The archive includes event photos, conference materials, lectures, workshops, educational programs, organizational files, and recovery documentation.

Digital Preservation Status

The archive has been verified with 805 files and approximately 56.75GB of preserved digital content. The portal is operational through GitHub Pages.

Prepared By

Prepared and organized by Peter Belarmino for professional archive access, review, and presentation.
