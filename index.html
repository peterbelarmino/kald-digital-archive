About the Digital Archive

The KALD Digital Archive Center was developed to preserve and organize historical media, documents, photographs, videos, and reports related to the Kuwaiti Association for Learning Differences.


This portal provides a professional access point for reviewing archived materials, supporting presentation, documentation, institutional memory, and future digital preservation.
